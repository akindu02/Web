Question 3

Here’s a quick, safe method:

  • Dim or turn off tank lights for 30–60 minutes.
  • Float the sealed bag in the tank for 15–20 minutes to equalize temperature.
  • Open the bag; add small amounts of tank water every 5 minutes for 20–30 minutes, or use a drip line (2–4 drops/sec for 45–60 minutes).
  • Net the fish into the tank—do not pour bag water into your aquarium.
  • Monitor closely for the first day; feed lightly.

Question 4

Typical targets (species may vary):

  • Freshwater: pH 6.8–7.4, temp 24–26 °C (75–79 °F), GH 6–10 dGH, KH 3–6 dKH.
  • Planted: CO₂ 20–30 ppm, nitrates 5–20 ppm, gentle flow and strong light.
  • Saltwater: 1.024–1.026 SG (≈35 ppt), temp 24–26 °C (75–79 °F), alkalinity 8–10 dKH.

We offer free in‑store water testing and can help you match parameters at home.
